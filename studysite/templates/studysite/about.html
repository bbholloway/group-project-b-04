About Us

Studdy Buddy is brought to you by Group B-04 of the Spring 2022 CS 3240 Class.

What is Study Buddy?


Study Buddy is a project designed to provide students with a more immersive and interactive study expeirence. We aimed to develop a web app that connects students with others in their courses in order to provide a network of resources, material, and management. Sign up, Create a Profile with all you classes, and be matched with other students in the same courses to study with. Create study parties for tests and exam, or even chill homework sessions. Study Buddy is here to help you be your best.
